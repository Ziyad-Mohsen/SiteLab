FAQs

Have questions? We’ve got answers! From service details and pricing to support and SEO, find out everything you need to know.

How much does a website cost?

The cost of a website depends on several factors, including the complexity of the design, the number of features, and the size of the project. We offer customized quotes based on your specific needs. Please contact us for a detailed estimate.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for completing a website can vary based on the project's scope and requirements. On average, a basic website can take 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects may take longer. We’ll provide a timeline estimate once we understand your project requirements.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your website remains up-to-date and secure. This includes software updates, security checks, and technical support. Contact us to learn more about our support packages.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we design are responsive and optimized for mobile devices. We ensure that your site looks and functions perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes, we build websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update text, images, and other content. We also provide training to help you manage your website effectively.

Will you help with domain registration and hosting?

Yes, we can assist with domain registration and hosting setup. We can recommend reliable hosting providers and manage the setup process for you.
